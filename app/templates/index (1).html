Written by the Stars
Custom Short Stories in the Style of William Shakespeare and Edgar Allan Poe

Product Spec

Team Solar Systems

  • The Project

    A project where you input your prompt and get out a short story of your choice: a tragedy from Edgar Allen Poe, or a comedy from William Shakespeare.

  • The Dataset

    We used various datasets to train our model. To train our model to write in the style of Poe, we used Edgar Allen Poe's Corpus of Short Stories and Edgar Allen Poe Complete Works. To write in the model of Shakespeare, we used Shakespeare's plays, the Complete Works of William Shakespeare, and The Complete William Shakespeare Plays. These datasets were selected and preprocessed because they pertained to our genre requirements for each author: tragedy for poe and comedy for shakespeare.

  • The Model

    For Shakespeare, we used the model distilgpt2, while for Poe we used the model gpt2.

  • Use Cases and Expansions

    This text generation can be used to simulate writing styles of two famous authors using your own keywords. This could be expanded to add more authors with unique writing styles.

  • Challenges Faced

    Finding datasets to use: These were hard to find because we only specifically needed comedies, not tragedies; we had to manually filter out these tragedies from the comedies in .txt files and there were also long training times, unknown errors, and balancing steps and temperature.
